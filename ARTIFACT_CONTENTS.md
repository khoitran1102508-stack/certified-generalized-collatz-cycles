# Artifact contents

- `certificates/`: final certificate and a small example certificate.
- `verifier/`: separate streaming verifier and row verifier.
- `src/`: exact-arithmetic certificate writer and divisor-threshold sieve implementation.
- `scripts/generate_certificate.py`: deterministic generation, verification, dataset, and comparison orchestration.
- `results/`: canonical cycles, row summary, comparison data, and recorded performance metrics.
- `data/reference/`: derived Wegner reference dataset and provenance notes.
- `docs/`: mathematical reduction, sieve, certificate format, and certified theorem statement.
- `tests/`: focused arithmetic, pruning, alignment, and adversarial validation tests.
- `.github/workflows/verify.yml`: automated example verification and the public test suite.
